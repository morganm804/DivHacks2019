A Womxn's Guide to Computer Science





Right now, only 18% of undergraduate Computer Science degrees are awarded to women. Even though women were instrumental in creating the field (women wrote both the first computer program and the first compiler), systemic issues have prevented them from entering the field. As technology becomes extremely relevant in our societal structure, it is essential that we have diverse voices in the room while creating technology. Explore this website to learn about the history of women in computer science, get some practice with HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and explore more resources to get involved!
